Juris Digital has officially acquired JurisPage from Uptime Legal, and we couldn't be more excited about what this means for the law firms we serve.

This isn't a rebrand or a pivot. It's a strategic combination of two companies that have spent years working exclusively with law firms — one focused on digital marketing, the other on website development and technology infrastructure. Together, we're building something that hasn't existed in the legal marketing space: a true end-to-end growth platform for law firms.

## Why This Acquisition Happened

Law firms have been forced to cobble together their digital presence from multiple vendors — one company for the website, another for SEO, another for paid ads, and yet another for hosting and IT. The result is fragmented strategies, finger-pointing when results don't materialize, and a lot of wasted budget.

JurisPage's website development capabilities and technology infrastructure paired with Juris Digital's marketing expertise eliminates that problem. One team. One strategy. One point of accountability.

## What This Means for Clients

If you're a current JurisPage client, the short answer is: things are getting better, not different.

Here's what the acquisition delivers:

- **Stronger integration between marketing and technology** — Your website, SEO, content, and paid campaigns will all be managed under one roof with a unified strategy
- **Deeper legal industry expertise** — Both organizations have served the legal sector exclusively. This isn't a generalist agency learning your industry on the fly
- **Expanded resources and support** — More specialists, broader capabilities, and faster response times across North America
- **Improved growth infrastructure** — Marketing strategies backed by secure, reliable cloud ecosystems designed specifically for law firms

## Built Exclusively for Law Firms

There are plenty of marketing agencies that will take a law firm's money. Very few of them understand the unique challenges attorneys face — YMYL content requirements, bar advertising rules, the competitive dynamics of local legal search, or why a "leads generated" report means nothing if those leads don't convert to signed cases.

Both Juris Digital and JurisPage were built from the ground up to serve law firms. That hasn't changed. If anything, this acquisition doubles down on that commitment.

As Casey Meraz, CEO of Juris Digital, put it: "Acquiring JurisPage strengthens our ability to build not only high-performing websites, but digital growth platforms that are supported by a deep understanding of how law firms operate."

## What Comes Next

We're already working on integrating the best of both companies. In the coming months, you'll see expanded service offerings, tighter alignment between website performance and marketing results, and new tools designed to help law firms grow more efficiently.

If you're a current client, your account team will be reaching out with specifics. If you're considering JurisPage for the first time, you're arriving at the right moment.
