## 1. The Core Offer

- **What it is:** "An affordable, efficient, and proven marketing solution for small law firms."
- **The headline on the source page:** "Launch Your Firm's Marketing and Gain New Clients Fast"
- **Subhead:** "Kickstart your law firm's growth with our fastest and most cost-effective digital marketing solution for small and startup law firms."
- **Problem it solves:** "Launching a new law firm is challenging enough without having to worry about online marketing. Many firms spend thousands without seeing results, or simply don't know where to start."
- **Target buyer:**
  - Small or startup law firms
  - Little to no online presence
  - Budget range: **$2,000–$4,000/month**
  - Practice-agnostic (no single PA focus)
- **The promise:** "You'll start seeing new leads within months."
- **Primary differentiator vs. larger Juris Digital tier:** Fully templated + automation-heavy; NOT a bespoke service. "Customization is limited to keep this service affordable."

## 6. FAQ (verbatim)

**Q: How can you offer such a competitive price and produce results so quickly?**
A: We designed LaunchPad to be affordable for startup law firms while delivering rapid results. We're able to offer this solution at an affordable price for two reasons:
1. Despite front-loading the most resource-intensive aspects of your campaign, we don't front-load costs. Instead, we spread those costs out over the course of your engagement.
2. We have fully embraced automation and generative AI. By strategically leveraging this technology – backed by our 12 years of legal SEO expertise – we're able to deliver highly effective marketing faster and cheaper.
Unlike some agencies, we do not charge a large up-front fee but we do front-load our efforts. Why? Because we know that small firms need access to their cash but we also understand the critical importance of speed when it comes to SEO. The faster we can start the faster you'll see results.

**Q: How much involvement will be required from me?**
A: We understand that you're busy so we've designed LaunchPad to require as little of your time as possible. We will need roughly 30 minutes of your time for a kickoff call. This is where we'll learn everything we need to know about your firm, your differentiating factors, your clients, your design and aesthetic preferences, and your key practice areas.
During the first 45 days we'll need you to respond promptly to our requests which will include signing off on design elements and content topics, as well as help verifying your Google Business listing and other local profiles. All in, this will likely amount to no more than a few hours of your time.

**Q: How much customization can I expect?**
A: We want to be clear – you are not investing in a bespoke service which is why we can offer such a competitive price and rapid results. That being said, your website will contain custom imagery, colors, marketing copy, and content, while leveraging our proven design layout. Customization is limited to keep this service affordable.

**Q: Who will I be working with?**
A: Your campaign will be driven by one of our lead strategists. You will have access to them via email – which will be responded to promptly – and you may always request a phone call.

**Q: Will I own my website and content?**
A: Yes, always. All IP we create for you is owned by you.

## 10. Known Deltas to Watch For in jurispage.com

When auditing, flag any of the following as potential misalignment:

1. **Contract terms.** Source says **24 months**. jurispage.com currently says "**Month-to-month**" and "**No contracts**" in multiple places (e.g., `app/best-law-firm-seo-companies/page.tsx:364`, `app/scorpion-legal-marketing-alternative/page.tsx`, `app/bing-ads-for-lawyers/page.tsx:307`, homepage hero copy). This is the biggest positioning inconsistency — decide which is canonical and make it match.
2. **Upper tier range.** Source says Juris Digital Established is **$5,000–$50,000+/mo**. jurispage.com says **$5,000–$20,000+/mo**. Likely safe to leave lower for positioning, but note the delta.
3. **Weekly social media posting.** Source explicitly names this as an included monthly deliverable. jurispage.com rarely mentions social media as a deliverable — check every service/pricing page.
4. **Brand Design deliverable.** Source includes logo, typography, photography, graphics, social media assets, brand guidelines as a Step-1 campaign-setup deliverable (not an add-on). jurispage.com currently treats logo design as a **$999 one-time add-on** in LaunchpadCalculator. This is a real inconsistency — either the source page includes logo in the base $2K (which makes $999 double-charging) or jurispage's calculator is correct and the source page is loose. Flag for resolution.
5. **StoryBranded website.** Source uses "StoryBranded" as a design philosophy. If jurispage.com doesn't use this phrase anywhere, that's a messaging gap.
6. **45-day setup timeline.** Source gives a specific 45-day setup window. jurispage.com MetroPage says "30 days from kickoff to launch." Delta.
7. **Automation / generative AI + 12-year expertise framing.** Source emphasizes these heavily as why the price is possible. Missing on jurispage.com.
8. **Client time commitment.** Source promises "30-minute kickoff + a few hours across 45 days." Missing on jurispage.com.
9. **"You own your website and content"** ownership guarantee. Source is explicit and names it in FAQ. jurispage.com has similar language but it's not as prominent.
10. **Target buyer language.** Source says "small or startup law firms… little to no online presence." jurispage.com sometimes says "1–4 attorneys" or "1–2 attorneys" — check consistency.
